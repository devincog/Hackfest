Mr. Clarke's Research Notes — Wireless Signal Interference in the Upside Down

CHAPTER 1: Electromagnetic Anomalies

The Upside Down appears to generate its own electromagnetic field that directly interferes with
standard radio communications. Observations from the Hawkins National Laboratory indicate that
proximity to dimensional rifts causes significant signal degradation in the 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz bands.

Key findings:
- CB radio transmissions become unreliable within 200 meters of a known gate location
- AM radio frequencies below 1 MHz show paradoxical signal amplification near rifts
- The interference pattern follows an inverse-square law relative to rift proximity

CHAPTER 2: The Demogorgon Effect on Electronics

Biological entities from the Upside Down emit a localized EMP-like field. This has been observed
to disable flashlights, car batteries, and telephone switching equipment. The field strength
appears proportional to the size and aggressiveness of the creature.

Laboratory measurements suggest:
- Field strength of approximately 50-100 V/m at 1 meter distance
- Pulse duration of 200-500 milliseconds
- Frequency spectrum centered around 60 Hz with harmonics extending to 10 kHz

CHAPTER 3: Communication Through Dimensions

Joyce Byers demonstrated that low-powered electrical circuits (specifically Christmas lights)
can serve as a rudimentary communication channel between dimensions. This suggests that the
dimensional barrier is permeable to low-frequency electromagnetic signals.

Theoretical implications:
- The barrier acts as a low-pass filter with a cutoff frequency around 100 Hz
- Modulated DC signals can penetrate where AC signals cannot
- Biological neural electrical activity (approximately 1-100 Hz) can also cross the barrier

CHAPTER 4: Protecting Equipment

Based on field observations, the following countermeasures have proven effective:
1. Faraday cage enclosures for sensitive electronics
2. Grounding systems using copper rods driven at least 8 feet into the earth
3. Surge protectors rated for at least 1000 joules
4. Backup power systems with isolated battery banks
5. Shielded coaxial cables for all signal transmission

CHAPTER 5: The Mind Flayer's Network

The Mind Flayer appears to utilize a biological neural network that operates on principles
similar to but distinct from electromagnetic radiation. This "psychic network" operates on
frequencies below 1 Hz and can penetrate all known shielding materials.

Properties of the psychic network:
- Operates in the 0.01 to 0.5 Hz range (infra-low frequency)
- Cannot be blocked by conventional Faraday cages
- Signal strength does not diminish with distance in the conventional sense
- Appears to use quantum entanglement-like phenomena for instantaneous communication
